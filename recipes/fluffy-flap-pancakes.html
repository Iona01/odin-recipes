Fluffy Flapjack Pancakes

Description

These flapjack pancakes are quick, easy, and turn out fluffy every time. The recipe can be easily adjusted to your dietary needs, too. I have tried many recipes; this one is simple, yummy, and fail-proof!

A word of caution: they're very filling.

NB: You can make this with all-purpose flour or whole wheat flour.

NB: Use dairy-free milk substitutes or water in place of milk if desired.
